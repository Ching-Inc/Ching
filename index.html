ABOUT US

Ching is a peer-to-peer application that enables users to make transfers anytime, anywhere & to anyone. Our goal is to create a network that allows sending and receiving money regardless of the payment platforms involved.

With our continuously growing partner network, we aim to expand the possibilities of where money can be sent, received, and requested.
