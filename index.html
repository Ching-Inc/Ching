About us

Ching is an upcoming payment service that enables users to make Cross-Platform transfers Anytime, Anywhere & to Anyone. Our goal is to make fund transfer as easy as possible

Roadmap

Phase 01

Allow customers to view transactions, add/remove payment account, and manage their profiles.

Enable sending funds from PayPal, Venmo, Apple Pay, and Google pay.

Enable receiving funds in PayPal and Venmo.

Phase 02

Onboard and integrate more P2P payment platforms.

Support other payment account types like bank account and credit/debit cards.
