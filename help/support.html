How to contact Ching Support

To report any issues, complaints, or bug, please send an email to support@chingtechnologies.com and we'll try to respond within 24 hours. You can also contact us from the Ching app by following the few steps below:

  • Open the Ching app and make sure you're signed in.
  • Tap on the profile icon on the top-left of the home screen.
  • A drawer menu will open with some navigation options. Tap on the "Support" drawer item to ope the support view.
  • On the Support screen, fill in your feedback/issue and select Submit. A support ticket will be opened for a team member to resolve.
