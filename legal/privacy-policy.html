    When you use the App, we collect personal information from: a. you directly when you provide it (e.g., when you enroll in the App, you provide your information to us); b. your device (e.g., your mobile device shares your Unique Device ID and name of your device when you use the App); c. third party mobile apps you link to your Ching account (e.g., you share account information with us); and d. our service providers (e.g., we may use service providers to help us verify your identity when you enroll in the App).

    We may share personal information with the following categories of third parties for the business purposes we described above in Section 5. a. other Ching users (e.g., When another user sends you a payment, we may share your name (from your Ching profile) and profile picture (if you have one) with the other user to ensure the other user intends to send a payment to you.   7. Minors’ privacy

    The Ching User Service Agreement defines eligibility requirements to use the App. Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children. We do not sell personal information collected from the App. Therefore, we would not sell information about minors. For information about the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), visit the FTC website: www.ftc.gov.

  8. Your choices

    You can control how and when you want to receive notifications from us by following the instructions within the notices you receive from us and by updating your preferences in the App. You may not opt-out of administrative emails (e.g., emails about your transactions or policy changes) for your enrolled profile. If you use the Ching app on an iOS device, you may delete your profile by selecting “Delete Account” in the app settings on your device. However, doing so will immediately terminate your ability to use the service. Please note that simply deleting the app from your device without selecting “Delete Account” (via the app’s settings) will not result in your Ching   profile being closed and deleted.  If you only delete the app from your device, (without changing the app’s settings) you will remain a registered user and Ching   will retain your data per our data retention policies.

  9. Updating your personal information

    You can update your personal information at any time by accessing your profile in the App. You can update your User profile and registration information, including your account information and email addresses. If your mobile telephone number has changed, you will need to contact the company directly by emailing support@chingtechnologies.com. Keeping your profile information up-to-date is very important. If this information is incomplete, inaccurate, or not current, please update your information by accessing your profile in the App.
